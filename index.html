Ratan Tata

" Indian Industrialist , Former chairman of Tata Sons "


G.O.A.T. RATAN TATA SIR

Here's a time-line of his Life...

Ratan Tata sir has devoted his whole life for his country. He is known for being a man of words, focus and commitment. He was once misbehaved by Bill Ford(Chairman of Ford) when he had gone to sell Tata Motors in 1999. Later in 2008, he bought two subsidiaries Land Rover & jaguar of Ford Motor Company.Although he is the son of very rich family, he lives in simplicity and is very down to earth in nature.Even after being so successful, Ratan Tata is still unmarried. Ratan Tata is fond of books and reading. He is also a very proficient pilot. He loves animals, especially dogs, very much. Spending his 21 golden years in Tata Group, he took Tata Group to the pinnacle of success.He has received many Indian and foreign awards and recognitions. Ratan Tata is also known for his charitable deeds. He donates about 75% of his income for social causes. He is a true human being with moral and ethics.

  • 1961Rejected IBM's offer-started with handling a blast furnace at Tata steel
  • 1981Became the Chairman of Tata Industries.
  • 1983Revolutionised the salt industry with the first iodised salt - "Tata Namak, Desh ka Namak"
  • 1986Became the Chairman of Air India
  • 1991Became the Chairman of Tata Group.
  • 1999His offer to sell Tata Motors to Ford Motors was rejected.(the irony!)
  • 2000Received the Padma Bhushan.
  • 2000Tata tea acquired Tetly Group-now the 2nd largest tea brand in the world.
  • 2004TCS went public: first India IT company to cross $1 Bn in revenue.
  • 2006Entered the DTH space with Tata Sky- now a leader in the space.
  • 2008Saved Ford Motors from bankruptcy - acquired Jaguar & Landrover.
  • 2008Received the Padma Vibhushan.
  • 2009Launched the cheapest car in India for the "Common man"- Nano for rs.1L.
  • 2010Donated $50 Mn to Harvard Business School for an executive centre - named Tata Hall in his honour.
  • 2012Retired, but kept the title of Chairman Emeritus. Grew Tata Group to over $100 Bn in revenues under his tenture.
  • 2014Made the largest donation in IIT Bombay's history - rs.95Cr for their R&D centre.
  • 2022Welcomed Air India back to Tata Group after 69 years.
